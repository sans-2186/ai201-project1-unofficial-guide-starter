- [Justcuriousyoung] Tom Henderson’s class is an easy A. He is really funny and easy to talk to. There are quizzes almost every week which are take-home open-note, plus an essay he grades light on.

- [Winter_Complaint8966] We get a cheat sheet on the midterm and final, and they are both in-class exams. There is an online quiz almost every week due on Sunday nights. There is also a group presentation and paper assignment. I feel like he is a great lecturer, but some of the class content lacks structure. Overall, the midterm was simple but I definitely took advantage of the cheat sheet.
