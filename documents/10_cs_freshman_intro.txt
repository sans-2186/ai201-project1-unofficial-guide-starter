- [OkIsland5453] For a freshman CS/SE major its fine. You are definitely not gonna have the most fun time, but its better to load up in your first year so that way when you start taking the harder classes you can actually make a schedule that doesn't leave you as swamped with classes all day like this one. Try to lessen the gaps though, unless you are a very productive person you are usually gonna waste all your time during them. Also try to take some classes at Collin over the summer if you really wanna make your life easier. It'll make your schedule a lot more manageable since you can justify taking the minimum credit hours needed to be full time (12 hours). For example, economics, government, history, or even CS classes for the first 2-3 years can be taken and their credit be transferred back to UTD. Just discuss those with your advisor first because classes with labs sometimes can't transfer. Note that transferred credits will not affect your GPA.
